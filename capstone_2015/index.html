Software Development Project - Semester 2, 2015

Welcome to the capstone software projects subjects. There are three of these: COMP3615, Software Development Project, COMP5615, Software Engineering Project and INFO3600, Major Development Project (Advanced). The common features of all are that students work in teams to create a software solution for an authentic client. We make use of XP, Extreme Programming, so that you will aim to quickly create useful and well tested small releases.

Clients play an essential role in these projects. They have generously agreed to invest their precious time in this enterprise. The teaching team will be active partners with the students, helping to ensure that students have an effective and valuable learning experience and to have the immense satisfaction of creating genuinely useful software product for their clients.

Welcome to this first subject in Human Computer Interaction (HCI). It has three strands. The first and main one follow the class text. It introduces the key aspects of designing and evaluating user interfaces, conventional (eg web-based applications) and the many emerging ways for people to interact with technology. The second technical strand builds skills to create web-based prototypes. The third stand is about hot-topic, new and emerging ways to interact with technology. Assumed knowledge is a background in programming and operating systems sufficient to independently learn new programming tools from standard online technical materials, ability to conduct a literature search and to write reports based on guidelines for technical writing.
