NOTE:
The individual grade acts similarly to the exam in other subjects.
To pass, you need to achieve at least 40%.
(Similarly, to be awarded a Credit, you need at least 55%, Distinction 65% and HD 75%).


Some examples:
A student has individual mark 39%, group mark 90%.
The individual mark is below the 40% required for a pass, the final mark is 49.

A student has individual mark 50%, group mark 90%.
as the individual mark is above the 40% for a pass but below the 55% required for a credit, the final mark is 64.

A student has individual mark 60%, group mark 90%.
The individual mark is above the 55% for a credit but below the 65% required for a distinction, the final mark is 64.
