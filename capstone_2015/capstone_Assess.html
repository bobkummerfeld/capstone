Calculating final mark

COMP/INFO


Following School of IT requirements, the individual grade acts similarly to the exam in other subjects.

How does this work? Your individual mark, expressed as a percentage + 10 is a cap on your final mark.

How is that calculated?

  1. Individual = individual mark (out of 30)
  2. Group = group mark (out of 70)
  3. Individual + Group
  4. Individual-as-percentage = Individual * 100/30
